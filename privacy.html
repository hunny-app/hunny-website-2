Privacy

Hunny Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 9, 2026

Overview

Hunny is a caregiver-managed mobile app with a child-facing companion experience. Adults create caregiver accounts, manage child profiles, pair child devices, and control settings. Children do not create standalone accounts. Before a caregiver creates a child profile, Hunny asks the caregiver for permission to use AI features and to send limited child data to the AI service provider described below.

Information Hunny May Collect

Hunny may collect caregiver account information, such as email address, display name, authentication provider, and account identifiers. Hunny may also collect household records, child profile details such as nickname, birthday or age band, caregiver relationship labels, profile settings, language settings, device pairing data, child session activity, conversation transcripts, voice transcripts, drawings, Snap Game photos when used, caregiver notes, tiny moments, subscription status, and caregiver or paired-device reports about specific AI responses.

Hunny collects this information when caregivers create or manage an account or child profile, when a child device is paired, when caregivers and children use chat, voice, drawing, Snap Game, family messaging, subscription, reporting, or settings features, and when Hunny records operational events needed to run and protect the service.

AI and Voice Features

Hunny uses Microsoft Azure OpenAI Service to provide AI chat, speech-to-text, synthetic voice, safety processing, AI response reporting support, and caregiver-facing summaries. Hunny may send child chat messages, recent conversation context, child profile details such as nickname, age band, caregiver relationship labels and language settings, voice transcripts or a short audio recording when fallback transcription is needed, AI response reports, and transcript or session context needed to generate caregiver summaries to Microsoft Azure OpenAI Service.

Hunny does not send Snap Game camera photos to Microsoft Azure OpenAI Service in the current version. Hunny does not keep raw child audio as long-term media history. Conversation transcripts may be stored so Hunny can support the child experience and create caregiver-facing summaries.

Advertising, Tracking, Location, and Contacts

Hunny does not sell personal information. Hunny does not use child data for advertising or cross-app tracking. Hunny does not offer purchases inside child mode. Hunny does not collect precise location or contacts in the current version.

How Hunny Uses Information

Hunny uses information to provide caregiver login, child-device pairing, child profile personalization, AI companion features, family activities, caregiver summaries, subscription access, AI response review, security, support, and account deletion. Hunny also uses limited operational information to maintain reliability, prevent abuse, debug problems, enforce access controls, and comply with legal obligations.

Service Providers

Hunny may use service providers such as hosting/database providers, authentication providers, Microsoft Azure OpenAI Service for AI and speech processing, RevenueCat, Apple App Store, Google Play, and operational infrastructure providers. These providers may process limited data needed to provide the service, maintain security and reliability, prevent abuse, process subscriptions, or comply with law.

Hunny requires service providers that process personal information for Hunny to provide the same or equal protection described in this policy and to process personal information only as needed to provide their services to Hunny.

Retention

Caregiver account, household, child profile, pairing, saved memory, and subscription records are generally kept until account or household deletion. Raw conversation transcripts are normally retained for up to 30 days, with a limited recovery window of up to 90 days when needed for summary processing. Other raw session events may be retained for up to 90 days. Snap Game photos may be kept until the session ends if not saved, or until household deletion if saved to Memories. AI response reports are generally kept until household deletion unless Hunny adopts a shorter review policy.

Caregiver Requests

Caregivers may request access, correction, or deletion by using in-app settings or contacting Hunny at hunny.app.official@gmail.com. You can also contact Hunny on Instagram at @hunny.kids.global.
