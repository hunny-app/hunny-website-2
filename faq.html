Hunny — Frequently Asked Questions

🧠 Understanding Hunny

1. What is Hunny?

Hunny is an AI-powered companion that checks in on your loved ones through natural conversations — and turns those interactions into simple updates and alerts for you.

Instead of constant monitoring, Hunny answers one question:

“Are they okay today?”

2. Who is Hunny for?

Hunny is designed for families who care about someone but can’t always be there:

  • Aging parents living alone
  • Busy families with children
  • Neurodivergent or autistic individuals
  • Loved ones dealing with emotional or mental health challenges
3. How is this different from other apps?

Most tools are:

  • ❌ Too invasive (cameras, trackers)
  • ❌ Too manual (calls, texts, spreadsheets)
  • ❌ Too clinical (medical, stressful)

Hunny is:

  • ✅ Non-intrusive
  • ✅ Conversation-based
  • ✅ Family-centered
  • ✅ Signal-focused (not diagnosis)

💛 Privacy & trust (biggest objections)

4. Is Hunny spying on my loved one?

No.

Hunny does not use:

  • Cameras
  • Constant tracking
  • Surveillance

It only learns from natural conversations, just like texting or chatting.

5. Will my loved one feel monitored or controlled?

No — Hunny is designed to feel like a friendly companion, not a tool.

This is critical because caregivers consistently reject tools that feel invasive or controlling.

6. Does Hunny make medical decisions or diagnoses?

No.

Hunny:

  • ❌ Does NOT diagnose
  • ❌ Does NOT replace doctors
  • ✅ Only provides signals and summaries

⚙️ How it works

7. What actually happens day-to-day?
  • Hunny checks in through casual conversation
  • It observes patterns (mood, routine, engagement)
  • You get a simple summary
  • You’re alerted only if something feels off
8. What kind of alerts will I get?

Only meaningful ones, like:

  • Missed routines
  • Sudden disengagement
  • Unusual behavior patterns

Not constant notifications.

9. What if nothing is wrong?

You’ll just see a calm update like:

👉 “Everything looks normal today.”

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family & care coordination

10. Can multiple family members use it?

Yes.

Hunny is built for care circles, so:

  • Everyone stays updated
  • No more chaotic group texts
  • Shared context in one place

(This is one of the biggest unmet needs caregivers complain about.)

11. Can I customize what I see?

Yes.

You can choose:

  • What data is shared
  • Who sees what
  • How often you get updates

🧩 Real problems it solves

12. Why do people actually need this?

Because caregivers today face 3 constant problems:

  • “Is something wrong?” (uncertainty)
  • “Am I doing enough?” (guilt)
  • “Why is this so hard to manage?” (friction)

Hunny reduces all three.

13. Why not just call or text them?

Because:

  • People say “I’m fine” even when they’re not
  • You can’t check constantly
  • It creates guilt on both sides

Families want signals, not guesswork.

14. Why not use existing apps?

Most people already try:

  • Notes
  • Spreadsheets
  • Group chats
  • Random apps

But they’re fragmented and exhausting.

Hunny replaces all of that with one clear system.

🚨 Risk / edge questions (high trust)

17. What if Hunny misses something?

Hunny is designed to:

  • Reduce risk
  • Provide early signals

But it is not a replacement for human care or emergency services.

18. What if my loved one doesn’t like using apps?

That’s exactly why Hunny exists.

It’s designed to:

  • Feel natural (conversation-based)
  • Require minimal effort
  • Avoid complex interfaces

Because many caregivers say:

👉 “My parent won’t use tech anyway.”

🌱 Why we’re building Hunny

19. Why did you build Hunny?

Because families aren’t asking for monitoring.

They’re asking for:

  • Peace of mind
  • Less guilt
  • A simple way to know their loved ones are okay

And that doesn’t exist yet.
