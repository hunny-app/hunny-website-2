Why Hunny

Other apps juggle multiple tasks — Hunny helps you enjoy family time.

Hunny is your personal family companion.

It checks-in on your family. It reminds you of your schedule and tasks when it’s time. No matter where or when.

Your Hunny is truly unique

Fully customizable, and it remembers the moments you share, your interests, your goals, and your dreams.

Hunny detects and alerts

Hunny detects subtle changes and patterns and reports them to you if something happens to your family.
